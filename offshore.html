MARITIME OFFSHORE SERVICES

Ojaf Marine provides a wide range of offshore support services, including anchor handling support for offshore construction vessels, accommodation
and ‘walk-to-work’ support for production platforms, we also offer chartering of barges and pontoons for transport services and workstations.

Support services for Offshore Support Vessels in port and in field: crew changing, bunkers, lubricants, parts and equipment, stores and goods.
Covering all sectors of the industry, including:

  • Dredging

  • Construction
  • Decommissioning
  • Drilling contractors
  • Engineering and procurement
  • Exploration and production
  • Floating production systems
  • Installation and commissioning
  • Offshore support vessels
  • Oilfield services
  • Subsea
  • Survey
  • Transportation and installation
  • Renewables
  • Salvage/emergency response
  • Underwater mineral mining
